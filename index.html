CHICKEN BIRYANI

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Basmati rice
  • Onions, tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices, herbs
  • Yogurt

Method

  1. Marinate chicken, cook with spices and vegetables.
  2. Layer rice and chicken, cook together until done.

Advantages

  • Rich taste, aromatic spices.
  • Provides proteins, carbohydrates.

Disadvantages

  • Time-consuming, high calorie.

ICE CREAM

Ingredients

  • Milk, cream
  • Sugar
  • Flavor (e.g. vanilla, chocolate)

Method

  1. Mix ingredients, churn and freeze till solid.

Advantages

  • Cools body, tasty treat.

Disadvantages

MASALA CHAI

Ingredients

  • Tea leaves
  • Spices (ginger, cardamom, cinnamon)
  • Milk, sugar

Method

  1. Boil water, add tea and spices.
  2. Add milk, sugar, simmer & serve hot.

Advantages

  • Refreshing, aids digestion.

Disadvantages

  • Caffeine content may affect sleep.
